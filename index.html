Established since 1984 with a small workshop producing aluminum grilles and decorative fences, Moradon has achieved success in expanding into the automotive parts industry

Starting 2009, we developed a new product related to lighting, both for households and industries. The product we introduced is LED technology-based lamps. We offer a wide range of product variations, including lamp designs, sizes, power options, spare parts, accessories, and, most importantly, at a more economical price compared to existing lamps.

Currently, PT Moradon Berlian Sakti has 300 trained employees, adequate equipment for both the domestic and international markets, and ISO 9001:2008 management system. We exist as one of the LED Light manufacturers in the national and international markets, particularly in Europe.

We provide solutions that:

Last long


Long lasting LED, providing up to 50,000 hours or 10 years of usage

Affordable


Carefully engineered high quality products within reasonable prices

Eco-friendly


We don't use mercury or other chemicals harmful to the environment
